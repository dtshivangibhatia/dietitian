Reverse PCOS / PCOD

Hormones are important for regulating most major bodily processes, so a hormone imbalance can affect a wide range of bodily functions.

Polycystic ovarian syndrome or disease (PCOS/PCOD) is a hormonal disorder that affects women in their reproductive age. PCOS is a common health condition that affects teen and young women, it is seen in 1 out of 10 women in their childbearing age. The reproductive system of women is controlled by the complex interplay of primarily five reproductive hormones namely estrogen, gonadotropin-releasing hormone, follicle stimulating hormone, progesterone and luteinizing hormone. An imbalance within these hormones leads to a hormonal disorder called the polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) or polycystic ovarian disease (PCOD) among women of reproductive age. Along with havoc in female hormonal control, ovaries release abnormal amounts of androgen (male hormones). This hormonal breakdown results in irregular or prolonged menstrual periods, excessive body hair, acne and hair loss. Polycystic ovaries may fail to develop follicles and regularly release eggs, causing difficulty in conceiving a pregnancy. Shivangi can assist you in the treatment of disorders caused by hormonal imbalances, such as PCOS/PCOD, diabetes and thyroid illness and the diet plan will be customized as per your taste preferences, local food availability and most importantly – your fitness goals.

Thyroid Management

With the rise of the industrial and packaged food industry we are also seeing a boom in metabolic diseases such as thyroid.

Hypothyroid is a medical condition in which the thyroid gland does not make enough thyroid hormone. Despite the fact that this hormone is necessary for growth, repair, and metabolism. As a result, hypothyroidism causes fatigue, dry skin, diarrhoea, and the ability to gain weight quickly. Hyperthyroid is a medical when your thyroid gland produces more thyroid hormones than your body requires, which is also known as hyperactive thyroid. The thyroid gland is a tiny butterfly-shaped gland located at the front of the neck. Thyroid hormones affect almost every organ in the body, including the rate at which your heart beats, as they manage how the body consumes energy. The thyroid usually causes slow metabolism. As per research, focuses on the right diet and exercise can help you boost your thyroid hormone activity. Shivangi’s goal is to correct metabolic derangements and reverse clinical progression as indicated with normal blood levels of thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) and free thyroxine (T4). The thyroid Management Plan is designed in such a way that a person can not only achieve weight loss but also fulfill the nutritional requirement as per the lifestyle and dietary preferences. The diet plan will be customized as per your taste preferences and availability of local food.

Post Natal / Lactation

Managing Nutrition or Losing the post-pregnancy weight can easily be one of the most difficult and dreadful things for a woman to do.

Post Natal period is the time when nutrient needs are greater in order to fulfill the baby’s needs along with you. You might want to lose weight post-delivery but you need to be patient enough so that your baby gets the correct nutrition in their crucial growth stage. Losing those extra pounds assumes topmost priority. Post-pregnancy days are filled with uncertainties and insecurities. Healing uterus, sleepless nights, and birthing scars. Although getting into the habit of eating healthy should always be followed, it is important to note that your body needs adequate time to recover from labor as well. A healthy diet is a key element of getting back in shape. You need to be careful about the food you intake after pregnancy. As a mother’s body needs proper nourishment to refuel the loss of various vitamins and minerals. During this phase, a lot of mothers face the problem of scanty milk supply and also the post-patrum depression is at bay.
