Thyroid Management

With the rise of the industrial and packaged food industry we are also seeing a boom in metabolic diseases such as thyroid.

Hypothyroid is a medical condition in which the thyroid gland does not make enough thyroid hormone. Despite the fact that this hormone is necessary for growth, repair, and metabolism. As a result, hypothyroidism causes fatigue, dry skin, diarrhoea, and the ability to gain weight quickly. Hyperthyroid is a medical when your thyroid gland produces more thyroid hormones than your body requires, which is also known as hyperactive thyroid. The thyroid gland is a tiny butterfly-shaped gland located at the front of the neck. Thyroid hormones affect almost every organ in the body, including the rate at which your heart beats, as they manage how the body consumes energy. The thyroid usually causes slow metabolism. As per research, focuses on the right diet and exercise can help you boost your thyroid hormone activity. Shivangi’s goal is to correct metabolic derangements and reverse clinical progression as indicated with normal blood levels of thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) and free thyroxine (T4). The thyroid Management Plan is designed in such a way that a person can not only achieve weight loss but also fulfill the nutritional requirement as per the lifestyle and dietary preferences. The diet plan will be customized as per your taste preferences and availability of local food.
